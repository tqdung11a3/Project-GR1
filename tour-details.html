Tour Schedule
DAY 1 | HCM city - Ha Noi

After gathering at Tan Son Nhat Airport, we board a morning flight to Hanoi. Upon arrival, our tour guide will welcome the group and take you to enjoy a traditional lunch featuring Northern Vietnamese cuisine.

In the afternoon, we begin our journey with a visit to Ba Dinh Square and the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum — a solemn and symbolic site of great historical value.

Next, we explore the One Pillar Pagoda and the Presidential Palace. Then, check-in at the hotel and relax. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner at a local restaurant and explore Hanoi’s Old Quarter on foot, soaking in the vibrant street life.

NGÀY 2 | HCM city - Ha Noi city

Start your day with breakfast at the hotel before heading to the Temple of Literature – Vietnam’s first national university and a symbol of learning and culture.

Continue with a guided tour of the Vietnam Museum of Ethnology, where you’ll discover the rich diversity of the country’s 54 ethnic groups.

In the afternoon, visit the iconic Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple. Enjoy a peaceful walk around the lake and take in the colonial architecture of the area.

Before dinner, don’t miss the chance to watch a traditional water puppet show — a unique Vietnamese cultural experience. Dinner at a local restaurant, followed by free time or optional night market exploration.
